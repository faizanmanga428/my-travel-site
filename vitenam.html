Tanzania: Safari & Zanzibar Adventure üáπüáø

Embark on an unforgettable safari adventure in the heart of Africa, with the option to relax on Zanzibar's pristine beaches. A truly unique and educational experience for all ages.

Visa Details & Cost

Visa Type: Electronic Visa (eVisa) is generally required for Indian and Pakistani passport holders.

Application Procedure: Submit application online via the official Tanzania eVisa portal. Receive approval via email and print it for arrival.

Required Documents: Valid Passport (min 6 months validity), UAE Residence Visa (min 6 months validity), photo, confirmed return ticket, proof of accommodation, sufficient funds.

Special Note for Pakistani Passport Holders: Tanzania is a "Referral Visa Category" country, meaning longer processing times (apply at least 3-4 weeks in advance).

Estimated Visa Fee: Approximately USD 50 - USD 100 per person.

Flight & Stay Costs

Flight Route: Dubai (DXB) to Kilimanjaro (JRO) or Dar es Salaam (DAR), potentially to Zanzibar (ZNZ) for beach extension.

Estimated Flight Cost (Return, per person): AED 1,800 - AED 3,000+.

Safari Lodges/Camps (per night for 5): AED 2,500 - AED 5,000+ (mid-range, due to all-inclusive nature).

Zanzibar Hotels (per night for 5, mid-range): AED 800 - AED 2,000+.

Total Estimated Accommodation (5-6 nights for 5): AED 10,000 - AED 30,000+.

September Weather & Key Experiences

September Weather: Excellent for safaris (dry season), clear, sunny, warm days, cooler nights. Good for Zanzibar outside main rainy seasons.

Places to Visit: Arusha (Safari Gateway), Tarangire National Park (elephants), Serengeti National Park (Great Migration, predators), Ngorongoro Crater (wildlife density), Zanzibar (beaches, Stone Town).

Things to Do: Game drives, optional hot air balloon safari, cultural visits, photography, beach relaxation, Stone Town tours, spice tours.

Sample Itinerary: Tanzania Safari & Zanzibar (6 Days)

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Arusha & Lodge Transfer. Arrive JRO, meet guide, transfer to safari lodge. Briefing, dinner, overnight.
  2. Day 2: Tarangire National Park Safari. Full day game drives, picnic lunch. Overnight near Tarangire.
  3. Day 3: Serengeti National Park (Internal Flight). Transfer to Arusha Airport for flight to Serengeti. Afternoon game drives. Dinner, overnight at camp.
  4. Day 4: Full Day Serengeti Game Drives. Explore endless plains. Optional hot air balloon safari. Track wildlife. Dinner, overnight.
  5. Day 5: Ngorongoro Crater & Transfer/Flight to Zanzibar. Early drive to Ngorongoro, crater game drive. Then, either drive back to Arusha OR fly to Zanzibar for beach resort.
  6. Day 6: Zanzibar Relaxation / Departure. If in Zanzibar, enjoy beaches/Stone Town, then depart. If in Arusha, souvenir shopping/local visit, then depart JRO.

Masai Mara, Kenya: Great Migration & Wildlife Safari üá∞üá™

Witness the spectacular Great Migration and abundant wildlife in one of Africa's most iconic reserves. An incredible adventure for wildlife enthusiasts.

Visa Details & Cost (for Kenya)

Visa Type: Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA) is mandatory for all foreign visitors, including Indian and Pakistani passport holders.

Application Procedure: Apply online via the official Kenyan eTA website. Receive approval via email and print it.

Required Documents: Valid Passport (min 6 months validity), UAE Residence Visa (min 6 months validity), photo, confirmed return ticket, proof of accommodation.

Processing Time: Typically 3-5 business days (apply at least 2 weeks in advance, especially for Pakistani passport holders).

Estimated eTA Fee: Approximately USD 34 - USD 50 per person.

Flight & Stay Costs

Flight Route: Dubai (DXB) to Nairobi (NBO), then internal flight to Masai Mara airstrips.

Estimated Flight Cost (Return, per person): AED 1,500 - AED 2,500 (international) + AED 800 - AED 1,500 (internal).

Safari Camps/Lodges (per night for 5, mid-range): AED 3,000 - AED 7,000+ (all-inclusive).

Nairobi Hotels (per night for 5, if staying): AED 700 - AED 1,500+.

Total Estimated Accommodation (5-6 nights for 5): AED 15,000 - AED 40,000+.

September Weather & Key Experiences

September Weather: Peak dry season, excellent for Great Migration and wildlife viewing. Sunny, pleasant days, cooler nights.

Places to Visit: Masai Mara National Reserve (wildlife, Great Migration), Nairobi (transit, Giraffe Centre, Sheldrick Wildlife Trust).

Things to Do: Game drives, Great Migration viewing, optional hot air balloon safari, Maasai village visits, photography.

Sample Itinerary: Masai Mara Safari (6 Days)

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi & Transfer to Masai Mara. Arrive NBO, connect to internal flight to Mara airstrip. Afternoon game drive, transfer to camp.
  2. Day 2-4: Full Day Game Drives in Masai Mara. Explore the reserve daily, seeking "Big Five" and migration. Bush lunches, cultural visits (optional).
  3. Day 5: Morning Game Drive & Return to Nairobi. Final morning game drive, then fly back to Nairobi. Optional Nairobi attractions. Overnight in Nairobi.
  4. Day 6: Nairobi Exploration & Departure. Explore Nairobi further (e.g., Karen Blixen Museum) if time permits, then depart NBO for Dubai.

The Philippines: Island Hopping & Tropical Paradise üáµüá≠

Discover breathtaking islands, pristine beaches, and vibrant marine life. Perfect for relaxation, island hopping, and water activities.

Visa Details & Cost

Visa Type: Generally requires a visa in advance for Indian and Pakistani passport holders.

Application Procedure: Typically through the Philippine Embassy/Consulate in the UAE. UAE residency can aid the process.

Required Documents: Valid Passport (min 6 months validity), UAE Residence Visa (min 6 months validity), form, photo, confirmed return ticket & hotel, bank statements, employer letter.

Estimated Visa Fee: Approximately AED 150 - AED 250 per person.

Flight & Stay Costs

Flight Route: Dubai (DXB) to Manila (MNL) or Cebu (CEB), then domestic flights to islands (e.g., Boracay, Palawan).

Estimated Flight Cost (Return, per person): AED 2,100 - AED 3,600+ (International + Domestic).

Boracay Hotels (per night for 5, mid-range): AED 700 - AED 2,000+.

El Nido Hotels (per night for 5, mid-range): AED 800 - AED 2,500+.

Total Estimated Accommodation (5-6 nights for 5): AED 3,500 - AED 10,000+.

September Weather & Key Experiences

September Weather: Towards the end of the wet season, warm and humid with intermittent rain. Still good for travel.

Places to Visit: Manila (transit, history), Boracay (white sands, lively), El Nido, Palawan (limestone karsts, lagoons).

Things to Do: Island hopping, water sports (snorkeling, diving), beach relaxation, sunset viewing, local cuisine, cultural exploration.

Sample Itinerary: Philippines Island Adventure (6 Days, Boracay Focus)

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Manila & Transfer to Boracay. Arrive MNL, connect to domestic flight to Caticlan. Transfer to Boracay, check-in, sunset on White Beach.
  2. Day 2: Boracay Beach Life & Water Sports. Enjoy White Beach, try parasailing, jet skiing, explore D'Mall.
  3. Day 3: Boracay Island Hopping Tour. Full-day tour to Puka Beach, Diniwid Beach, Magic Island, snorkeling.
  4. Day 4: Explore Beyond White Beach & Sunset Sailing. Visit Bulabog Beach, optional Mount Luho. Enjoy a Paraw sunset cruise.
  5. Day 5: Return to Manila / Free Time. Morning in Boracay, flight back to Manila. Optional visit to Intramuros or shopping. Overnight in Manila.
  6. Day 6: Manila Exploration & Departure. Explore Manila (museums, malls) if time permits, then depart MNL for Dubai.

Vietnam: Culture, Cuisine & Natural Beauty üáªüá≥

Discover Vietnam's vibrant culture, breathtaking natural landscapes, and incredible cuisine. From bustling cities to serene bays, it offers a rich and diverse experience.

Visa Details & Cost

Visa Type: eVisa generally required for Indian and Pakistani passport holders.

Application Procedure: Online application via official Vietnam eVisa portal. Generally straightforward and efficient.

Required Documents: Valid Passport (min 6 months validity), UAE Residence Visa (beneficial), photo, confirmed return ticket.

Processing Time: Typically 1-2 weeks (apply in advance).

Estimated Visa Fee: Approximately USD 25 - USD 50 per person.

Flight & Stay Costs

Flight Route: Dubai (DXB) to Hanoi (HAN) or Ho Chi Minh City (SGN).

Estimated Flight Cost (Return, per person): AED 1,500 - AED 2,500.

Daily Expenses (Accommodation, Food, Activities for 5, mid-range): AED 1,500 - AED 2,500 per person (excluding international flights).

Overall Cost: Generally very affordable, offering excellent value.

September Weather & Key Experiences

September Weather: End of rainy season, beginning of autumn in North (pleasant). Central/South may still have some intermittent showers.

Places to Visit: Hanoi (culture, history), Ha Long Bay (limestone karsts, cruises), Ho Chi Minh City (bustling, history), Hoi An (ancient town), Da Nang (beaches).

Things to Do: City tours, bay cruises, explore ancient towns, enjoy world-renowned cuisine, relax on beaches, visit historical sites.

Sample Itinerary: Vietnam (6 Days, North Focus)

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Hanoi & City Exploration. Arrive HAN, transfer to hotel. Explore Old Quarter, Hoan Kiem Lake.
  2. Day 2: Hanoi Cultural Tour. Visit Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, Temple of Literature, Ethnology Museum. Enjoy a water puppet show.
  3. Day 3: Ha Long Bay Cruise. Drive to Ha Long, board cruise. Explore karsts, caves, kayaking. Overnight on boat.
  4. Day 4: Ha Long Bay & Return to Hanoi. Morning activities on bay, brunch, disembark. Drive back to Hanoi.
  5. Day 5: Hanoi Free Day / Day Trip. Explore more of Hanoi or optional day trip (e.g., Ninh Binh/Tam Coc).
  6. Day 6: Departure from Hanoi. Enjoy a final Vietnamese breakfast, then transfer to HAN for flight back to Dubai.
